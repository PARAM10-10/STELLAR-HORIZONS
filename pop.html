About Stellar Horizons

Founded by former NASA engineers and space enthusiasts, Stellar Horizons is pioneering safe, accessible space tourism for adventurous spirits around the globe.

Our state-of-the-art spacecraft and rigorous safety protocols ensure that your journey to the stars is not only magical but also secure. We believe that space should be accessible to everyone who dreams of touching the cosmos.
